Brian Bailey, a Washington, D.C.-based web producer and technical writer.

In his travels, he's been fortunate enough to have worked at Pew Research Center, AARP, Lockheed Martin, U.S. News & World Report, The Association of Public Health Laboratories, The Export-Import Bank of the United States, and even the U.S. Army (which feels like a lifetime ago).

He occasionally spends his evenings doing freelance coding projects for friends and reading every free classic he can find in the Google Play ebook store. When there is still gas left in the tank, he marathons and bike races for both The Bike Rack Multisport Team and NCVC/UnitedHealthcare.
